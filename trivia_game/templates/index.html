How to Play

  1. First player (Chooser) selects a movie from our database.
  2. Second player (Guesser) tries to identify the movie.
  3. The Guesser gets 9 attempts to guess the movie correctly.
  4. After each incorrect guess, a new trivia fact about the movie is revealed.
  5. Trivia facts start difficult and get easier as the game progresses.
  6. Win by guessing the movie before running out of attempts!
